About PayVerify Nepal

An AI-driven solution built to protect small shop owners across Kathmandu Valley from payment screenshot fraud.

The Problem

Small businesses in Nepal increasingly rely on eSewa and Khalti for digital payments. However, many shop owners verify payments by simply looking at a screenshot — which can be easily edited or faked. This leads to real financial losses for honest sellers.

Our Solution

PayVerify Nepal uses Optical Character Recognition (OCR) to read the contents of a payment screenshot, then applies rule-based checks and a machine learning model to flag inconsistencies that indicate a fake or edited screenshot.

Privacy First

All verification happens locally. Screenshots and results are stored only for your own account and are never shared with third parties. No real banking credentials are ever requested or stored.

Who It's For

Designed for small shop owners aged 25–55 in Kathmandu Valley who accept eSewa and Khalti payments daily and need a fast, reliable way to confirm that a payment screenshot is genuine before handing over goods or services.

Technology Stack
Tesseract OCR Text extraction from screenshots
Scikit-learn Random Forest fraud classifier
Flask Python web framework
OpenCV Image preprocessing
